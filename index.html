    Lead Designer
    (Swipe to navigate)
  • The initial version of Data.gov as launched in 2009. The original design lacked a strong visual hierarchy. Primary user interactions including search were no easier to find than earthquake data.
  • The General Services Agency's initial redesign borrowed content display models from sites like Pinterest. User testing continued to indicate navigation issues. (DigitalGov case study)
  • The final design eliminated most of the visual distractions in favor of honing in on the most important user interactions. (Data.gov)
